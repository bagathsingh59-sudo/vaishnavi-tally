🧾 Export for CA / Audit

Downloads a .zip of Excel-readable CSV files your Chartered Accountant needs to audit the books & file returns:

  • Trial Balance
  • Day Book (all entries)
  • Profit & Loss
  • Ledger list with balances
  • Client balances
⬇️ Download Audit Pack (.zip)

💡 What is a CA audit? At year-end (or quarterly), a Chartered Accountant reviews your books to confirm everything is recorded correctly and to file your income-tax return. They need the Trial Balance, Profit & Loss and the list of all transactions (Day Book) — exactly what the Audit Pack gives. Just email them the .zip.
